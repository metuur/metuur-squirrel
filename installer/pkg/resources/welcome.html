🐿 Squirrel — Focus & Productivity Companion

This installer sets up everything in one step:

  • Squirrel.app — the desktop tray popup, into your Applications folder
  • squirrel command-line tool, for terminal access to your vault
  • Agent integration — skills & commands for any AI coding agent you have installed (Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, Windsurf)
  • A starter config and vault folder, ready to use

Nothing runs in the background until you open Squirrel. You can uninstall any time by dragging Squirrel.app to the Trash.
