Open Squirrel from your Applications folder (or Spotlight) to finish setup — it'll walk you through pointing at your vault.

Recommended setup

  • Allow notifications so reminders appear: System Settings → Notifications → Squirrel → turn on Allow Notifications (style: Banners or Alerts).
  • Launch at login so Squirrel is always running: enable it on Squirrel's first-run setup screen, or in System Settings → General → Login Items.

Using an AI coding agent? Type /sq-status in Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, or Windsurf to see your vault at a glance.
